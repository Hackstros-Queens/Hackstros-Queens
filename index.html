Trajectories

As you probably noticed, all objects on Earth (including you!) are subjected to gravity. The classical laws of physics clearly describe the movement of objects in those conditions; we can easily obtain their trajectory. Here, we explore the different parameters that dictate the trajectory of an object and we observe the effect of their variation.
